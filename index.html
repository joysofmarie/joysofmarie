About

Hi, I'm Marie

I'm a Purdue University student, creator, and future founder with one big belief: women shouldn't have to dress down to be taken seriously at work.

Joysofmarie is the beginning of something bigger — a community, a conversation, and eventually a brand built around quality clothing that's good for women and good for the earth.

Color as confidence — your palette is your voice. Use it.
Sustainable by design — secondhand, slow fashion, quality over quantity.
Women first — made for real bodies, real careers, real lives.
